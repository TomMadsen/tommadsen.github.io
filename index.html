  • In 2006 I graduated from Griffith University with a degree in Criminology, majoring in Chinese Language. At the end if that year, I joined the police. I held roles as a general duties officer in Brisbane and North Queensland.
  • In 2011 I took a year off with my wife to travel the world - From the Americas, to Africa, then from Europe to Asia. Highlights were:
    • Jumping out of a helicopter in the Swiss Alps
    • Camping in the Serengeti
    • Horse-trekking through the Mongolian Steppes
    • Learning to dive in Thailand
    • Experiencing St Patrick's day in Dublin
  • In 2012 I commenced work in the South Brisbane Criminal Investigation Branch as an investigator
  • In 2016 I made Detective - and saw it all - murder, torture, sexual assaults, drug trafficking...
  • In 2018 I left the police to take my family travelling around the world, travelling from Indonesia to Malaysia, to Thailand and Cambodia, to Sri Lanka then to Dubai then to Europe - Portugal, Spain, Italy and Croatia.
  • In 2019 I began studying photography and web-development. It was also here that due to a family emergency I returned home - to Gold Coast, Australia.
